This visualization explores data from the medicare disparity database, a collection of county-level health measures tracked by the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS). It is presented as an alternative to the mapping tool created by the center.

This is a proof of concept demonstrating different approaches to data exploration. It contains only a tiny slice of the complete dataset from 2019, as the CMS does not provide access to the data through a public-facing API.
